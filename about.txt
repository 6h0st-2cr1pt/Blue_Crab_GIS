<h2>Blue Crab GIS</h2>
<p>A comprehensive Geographic Information System designed for monitoring and analyzing Blue Crab populations in Negros Occidental.</p>

<h3>Features:</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Interactive GIS mapping with multiple view options</li>
    <li>Data analytics with charts and visualizations</li>
    <li>Dataset management and filtering</li>
    <li>CSV import and manual data entry</li>
    <li>Customizable settings</li>
</ul>

<h3>Developer Information:</h3>
<p>This application was developed by Jonald Sabordo as a tool for marine biologists and environmental researchers to track and analyze Blue Crab populations in Negros Occidental.</p>

<p>Contact: your.jonaldsabordo@gmail.com</p>

<h3>Technical Details:</h3>
<p>Built with PyQt5, Folium, Matplotlib, and SQLite.</p>
